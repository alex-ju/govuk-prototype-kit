Licence

Application to licence premises for massage or other special treatments

If you run an establishment for 'special treatments' (beauty treatments) you may require a licence for the establishment. Operators will also need to be suitably qualified to carry out treatments.

Special treatments include:

  • massage
  • manicure
  • chiropody
  • light treatments, eg sunbeds
  • electric treatments, eg electrolysis
  • other treatments involving heat, light or vapour, eg sauna or other bath treatments

You will be required to apply for your licence every year.
